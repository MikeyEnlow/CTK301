Formed in high school in Central Illinois, alternative rock band UNCHAiNED has captivated audiences with their high-energy live shows and nostalgic 90s alternative sound. Their expansive catalog blends rock, ska, funk, and reggae, creating an infectious appeal that keeps fans dancing and their hands in the air.

The band faced significant challenges in 2023 during the recording of their second EP. They suffered the tragic loss of their esteemed producer, Camron Yates, in a sudden car accident.

This heartbreak was compounded when lead singer Dave Allen lost his father in a similar accident just months later. Despite these profound setbacks, UNCHAiNED's unity and dedication to their music have only deepened.

Emerging from this period of adversity, they now channel their experiences into their craft with renewed passion and focus. Their performances and recordings are more vibrant than ever, reflecting a purposeful energy that resonates deeply with audiences, ensuring each show is a celebration of resilience and joy.
